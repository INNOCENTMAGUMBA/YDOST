Walled-Garden Segmentation

Prevent Congestion.
Maintain Relevance.

With 300M+ global migrants, platform congestion is a real risk. YurtDostluk solves this with strict walled-garden architecture: users only see content, services, and communities relevant to their specific Home→Host corridor.

  • Auto-Routing by Corridor

    Users are dropped into their specific Home→Host hub upon registration. No cross-corridor noise.

  • Nightly Cron Job Segmentation

    Automated jobs route users into niche groups by city, visa status, university, and profession—keeping feeds high-signal.

  • Visa-Free Corridor Detection

    Dynamic database of bilateral visa agreements. If your corridor is visa-free, you get immediate full access.

  • Progressive Profile Enrichment

    Pre-visa users complete basic demographics & vertical interests. Upon verification, profiles auto-populate for personalized recommendations.
