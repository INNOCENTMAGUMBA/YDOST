Platform Features

Everything Diaspora Communities Need, In One Place

From academic support to embassy services, YurtDostluk formalizes informal economies and builds trusted digital infrastructure for 300M+ global migrants.

Zero-Retention KYC

Bank-grade identity verification with GDPR/KVKK compliance. Passport/visa metadata extracted via AI — original documents permanently deleted after validation.

Dual-Party Escrow Engine

Secure transactions for tutoring, housing, proxy shopping & services. Funds held safely until service completion — eliminating scams in informal diaspora economies.

Campus-Link EdTech

Auto-routed academic cohorts by university → faculty → course. Peer tutoring marketplace, scholarship portals, and alumni networks — all verified and escrow-protected.

Embassy Command Center

B2G SaaS dashboard for real-time citizen census, mass broadcasts, per-diem tracking, SOS escalation, and democratic voting modules — all embassy-controlled.

Vertical Marketplaces

Discover & book verified providers across Education, Healthcare, Tourism & Commerce — all filtered by your corridor, visa type, and TrustScore ratings.

Trust-Point Economy

Gamified reputation system where verified contributions earn points redeemable for perks, visibility, or donations — incentivizing community helpfulness.
