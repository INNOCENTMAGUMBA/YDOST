Building the Global Civic Home
for Diaspora Communities

YurtDostluk ("Home of Friendship") is a KYC-verified Super-App that digitizes, formalizes, and protects the entire expat and international student lifecycle — connecting trusted services, academic support, embassy integration, and cross-border commerce in one secure platform.

Our Mission

To eliminate diaspora exploitation, academic fragmentation, and bureaucratic inefficiency

By providing a KYC-verified, escrow-protected, and institutionally integrated digital infrastructure that empowers millions of expats to live, work, study, and return home safely, productively, and with dignity.

Trust Through Verification

No anonymous accounts. Every user is cryptographically tied to a verified passport/visa/Kimlik identity.

Formalize Informal Economies

Convert cash-only, unregulated micro-services into trackable, escrow-backed, and rated transactions.

Institutional Integration

Bridge the gap between grassroots communities and official entities like embassies, universities, and immigration authorities.

Our Core Values

Principles That Guide Every Decision

These foundational principles ensure YurtDostluk remains secure, inclusive, and impactful as we scale globally.

Zero-Retention Privacy

Inspired by leading financial platforms, identification documents are never stored. Only extracted metadata and expiry dates are retained, drastically reducing breach liability while maintaining compliance with GDPR, KVKK, and CCPA.

Super-App Retention Model

Combine high-frequency daily utility (academic collaboration, social forums) with high-value transactional layers (escrow, logistics, housing) to guarantee sustained Daily Active User metrics and long-term engagement.

Pre-Visa Accessibility

Enable users to begin their journey with passport-only registration, upgrading access upon visa verification or automatic detection of visa-free corridors. No waiting for documentation to get started.

Dynamic Corridor Hubs

Prevent platform congestion with walled-garden segmentation. Users only see content relevant to their specific Home-Host pair, with nightly cron jobs auto-routing users into niche groups by city, visa, and profession.

Decentralized Governance

Community leaders and embassy staff handle Tier 1-2 disputes and moderation. The TeeParkots Tech only intervenes for "Tier 3 Escalations" (major fraud or legal requests), enabling scalable growth without massive centralized headcount.

Trust-Point Economy

Gamified reputation system where verified contributions earn points redeemable for perks, visibility, or donations — incentivizing community helpfulness and creating a self-sustaining ecosystem of trust.

The original concept was born from firsthand experience navigating the complexities of international migration: the struggle to find trusted translators, the absence of formal academic support networks, the reliance on informal hand-carry logistics, and the critical lack of real-time embassy coordination.

The core vision remains unchanged: to build an official, easy, and secure communication and service ecosystem that connects diaspora citizens with each other, their home authorities, host-country institutions, and willing supporters worldwide.
