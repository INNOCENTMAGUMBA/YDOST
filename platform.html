Zero-Retention KYC Protocol

Bank-grade identity verification that prioritizes privacy and compliance. Inspired by leading financial platforms, YurtDostluk never stores raw identification documents.

  • AI-Powered OCR Extraction: Only essential metadata (name, DOB, nationality, expiry dates) is extracted and encrypted.
  • Zero Image Retention: Physical ID images are permanently deleted immediately post-validation—no honeypot for hackers.
  • Compliance by Design: Fully compliant with GDPR (EU), KVKK (Türkiye), and CCPA (US) through data minimization principles.
  • Liveness Verification: Real-time biometric selfie matching prevents stolen identities and deepfake usage.

Dual-Party Escrow Engine

Eliminate fraud in informal diaspora economies. Every transaction—tutoring, housing, proxy shopping, logistics—is protected by institutional-grade escrow.

  • Secure Funding: Buyer deposits funds via card, mobile money, or bank transfer. Funds held in licensed escrow until service completion.
  • Transparent Terms: All agreements negotiated inside encrypted inbox with message logging for dispute resolution.
  • Instant Release: Upon confirmed delivery/completion, funds release instantly minus 3-5% platform fee.
  • Dispute Resolution: Either party can freeze escrow. Global Council reviews cryptographic evidence and mediates fairly.

Trust & Security

Built for Trust, At Scale

Every feature is designed with security, privacy, and compliance at its core.

Zero-Data Retention

Raw ID images are permanently deleted after AI metadata extraction. Only encrypted text strings and expiry dates are stored—minimizing breach liability while maintaining compliance.

Licensed Escrow Partners

All financial flows processed through licensed gateways (Stripe Connect, Iyzico) with built-in AML/CTF screening. Maximum transaction limits prevent capital flight misuse.

Cryptographic Voting

Homomorphic encryption enables tallying votes without ever decrypting individual choices. Zero-knowledge proofs ensure voter anonymity while guaranteeing eligibility.

Dynamic Compliance Layer

Modular compliance framework adapts to local laws (KVKK in Türkiye, GDPR in EU). Corridor-agnostic architecture enables rapid deployment across jurisdictions.

Full Audit Trails

Every transaction, vote, and data access logged with timestamp, user identity, and action details. Available for embassy review and regulatory compliance audits.

Decentralized Governance

Community leaders and embassy staff handle Tier 1-2 disputes. The TeeParkots Tech only intervenes for "Tier 3 Escalations" (major fraud or legal requests), enabling scalable growth.
